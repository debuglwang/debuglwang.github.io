About Me

This is what I have done with my life.

I used to work at a fried chicken place and worked hard hours. The place also had no air conditioning or heating so it was brutal. I made a lot of friends though, and I look back on those times fondly. This job also helps me pay for college. I took on side jobs fixing RC cars and cleaning and maintaining road bikes.

I started to wrangle alligators when I lost my teddy bear to a small alligator. I really abhor those gators!

I do a good deal of volunteering. Back home in Canada, I volunteered at the bishop's storehouse with my Mom and Dad. I also volunteered at food shelters. I really like helping out those less fortunate than I am.
